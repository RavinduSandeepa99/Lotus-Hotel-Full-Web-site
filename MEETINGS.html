MEETINGS AT LOTUS HOTEL SRI LANKA

Set amidst enchanting coastal views and conveniently located within easy reach of the commercial capital Colombo, Lotus Hotel is the perfect choice for corporate meetings and retreats. Featuring versatile indoor venues that can be set up to your requirements, be it large scale conferences to private corporate workshops, our meeting hotel in Sri Lanka provides a full range of facilities, services and catering options. One of the most conveniently located hotels near Colombo Airport, Sri Lanka we are a leading venue for MICE (Meetings, Incentives, Conferences, Exhibitions) events as well, we also provide the option of hosting functions outdoors by the pool, ideal for receptions or cocktail parties after a long day of meetings.

MEETINGS

Lotus Hotel offers modern meeting venues in Sri Lanka with convenient access to the city and facilities for all occasions.

If you’re looking at an indoor event, Lotus Hotel has two conference halls – Ekwin and Pehara, with seating capacities of 40 and 75 respectively. Both the halls are equipped with modern facilities and are ideal for day conferences, press conferences, and workshops at this airport hotel in Colombo.

We also have a spacious garden area complete with a pleasing environment and scenic views, perfect to organize outdoor events of your choice.

MICE

Looking for all inclusive MICE venues in Sri Lanka? At Lotus Hotel, we have spectacular indoor and outdoor locations suited to hold any MICE event. Our meeting rooms have been built to high standards with modern equipment and facilities geared to hold high quality events.






MEETING VENUES

If you are looking for an event venue in Sri Lanka to suit your specific needs, get in touch with us. Situated in between the Bandaranayake International Airport and the Colombo city, our venues are ideal for gatherings away from the bustle of city life.
